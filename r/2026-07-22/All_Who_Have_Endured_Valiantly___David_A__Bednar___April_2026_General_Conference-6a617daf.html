In this April 2026 General Conference message, Elder David A. Bednar presents enduring to the end as an ongoing spiritual transformation rather than simply pushing through hardship or duties. He teaches that disciples become new creatures in Christ as their character, desires, actions, and love are aligned with God's will through the Savior, covenants, and the Holy Ghost.

What this video offers

"Endure to the end" is framed as a lifelong mighty change of heart, not mere perseverance through demanding circumstances.

The goal is to become new creatures in Christ, not only to improve outward behavior.

Charity is presented as central to becoming more like the Savior and to enduring valiantly.
